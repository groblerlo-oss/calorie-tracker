🥗 Meal Prep Tips

For Weight Loss:

  • Prep grilled chicken breasts (165 cal each)
  • Boil eggs in bulk (78 cal each)
  • Cut veggies for easy snacking (5-55 cal)
  • Keep protein shakes ready (120 cal)
  • Meal prep wraps for quick lunches

For Weight Gain:

  • Cook rice & pasta in bulk (200+ cal/cup)
  • Prep high-calorie wraps & sandwiches
  • Make protein shakes with milk (200+ cal)
  • Add nuts to every meal (200 cal/oz)
  • Always have mass gainer ready
